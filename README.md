## Business Problem

Many small and medium-sized organizations rely on spreadsheets, manual processes, or expensive third-party HR software to manage employee information. These approaches can lead to:

    - Data duplication
    - Inefficient workflows
    - Limited access control
    - Difficulty tracking employee records
    - Increased administrative costs

The Internal HR Manager provides a centralized and secure solution for managing employee and department information. By developing an in-house HR management system, organizations can reduce dependence on costly commercial HR platforms, lower operational expenses, and customize features to fit their specific business needs.
